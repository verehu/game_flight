# Enemy Archetypes

## Wave Composition Targets

- Sector A: introduce new enemies at low frequency to teach behavior.
- Sector B: increase mixed pressure and add one elite type.
- Sector C: maintain readable chaos with all archetypes active.

| Enemy | Role | Threat | Sector A | Sector B | Sector C |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `scout` | Fast interceptor, basic pressure | Low | 50 | 36 | 24 |
| `striker` | Lane tracker + single shot | Medium | 25 | 24 | 22 |
| `tank` | High HP spread shooter | Medium-high | 8 | 14 | 18 |
| `swarm` | Light zig-zag crowd filler | Low | 10 | 11 | 10 |
| `charger` | Telegraph then dash | High | 4 | 6 | 9 |
| `sniper` | Slow aim control + precision shot | High | 2 | 5 | 8 |
| `guard` | Orbiting escort + dual aimed shot | High | 1 | 3 | 6 |
| `ufoElite` | Mini-elite burst shooter | Very high | 0 | 1 | 3 |

## Readability Rules Per Type

- `swarm`: smallest silhouette and fastest spawn, no special bullet pattern.
- `charger`: bright telegraph tint before any dash.
- `sniper`: keeps standoff zone so aimed bullet origin is readable.
- `guard`: orbit movement creates clear lane denial zone.
- `ufoElite`: unique silhouette (`ufoRed`) and denser burst profile.
